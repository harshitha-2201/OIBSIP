AboutUs

Shikhar Travels, a complete Destination Management Company and adventure tour specialist, provide comprehensive travel packages for adventure, leisure, group, corporate as well as individual travellers. Ever since its inception in 1979, the company has been growing steadily and in the process innovating its product range, launching new ideas and rediscovering new destinations to increase its service range.

At Shikhar we are adaptable to our client’s ideas and specific requirements which has helped us to become one of the most sought after travel companies in the country, providing excellent support to individual and group travellers, catering to their specific requirements.

Besides domestic travellers, we also cater to different markets from North America to East-Europe, and from Asia Pacific to Australia . We work closely with our partner companies across the world to a mutually beneficial business association with them and assure our best travel solutions and experience to their clients.

Trending Indian Destinations

India is a country with diversified travel destinations. We have mastered various holiday options to make our customer experiences more memorable.

Goa

LGoa Water Sports Tour, a package of 3 nights and 4 days, offers you a chance to indulge in water sports at some of the very well-known beaches of North Goa. You also indulge in fun activities. During the tour, you visit the well-known churches of Goa.

Andaman

With white sandy beaches, turquoise waters and beautiful sunset views, Andamans is a great destination for a perfect laid-back vacation. These Andaman luxury packages for 4 nights 5 days lets you explore the beauty of the island and also visit some of the best attractions on the Island.

Mysore

With spectacularsnowy peaks and plunging river valleys, beautiful Himachal isIndia’s outdoor adventure playground. From trekking and climbing to rafting, paragliding and skiing, if it can be done in the mountains, it can be done here

Strung out along a ridge with steep forested hillsides falling away in all directions, the Himachal capital is one of India's most popular hill resorts, buzzing with a happy flow of heat-escaping Indian vacationers. Traffic is banned from the central part of town, so walking is pleasant

Srinaga

Ringed by an arc of green mountains, Srinagar's greatest drawcard is mesmerizingly placid Dal Lake, on which a bright array of stationary houseboats and shikara (gondola-like boats) add a splash of color and a unique opportunity for romantic chill-outs.
